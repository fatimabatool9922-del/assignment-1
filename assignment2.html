About Black Leopards

A black leopard (also known as a black panther) is a leopard with a dark coat caused by a genetic condition called melanism. Despite their dark color, their rosette spots are still visible in the sunlight.

Interesting Facts

  • They are found in Africa and Asia.
  • They are excellent climbers and hunters.
  • Melanism makes them darker than normal leopards.
  • They are very rare in the wild.
