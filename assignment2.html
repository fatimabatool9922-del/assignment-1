The Mysterious Panther

A black leopard is known as a black panther with a dark coat caused by a genetic condition called melanism.Despite their dark colour,their rosette spots are still visible in the sunlight

Interesting facts

  • They are found in africa and asia.
  • They are excellent climbers and hunters.
  • Melanism makes them darker then normal leopards.
  • They are very rare in the wild.
